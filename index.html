About Us

Gini Enterprise is a registered Indian export company based in Pune, Maharashtra, specializing in premium-quality agricultural and botanical products
Gini Enterprise is committed to delivering purity, consistency, and reliability to global markets.

We source directly from trusted farming communities and process products in facilities that meet international food safety standards. Each product is ethically produced, lab-tested, and fully certified for export.

OUR MISSION

empower farmer communities through direct procurement and fair trade partnerships
enhance product quality through advanced processing, testing, and hygienic packaging
build long-term relationships with international buyers through trust, transparency, and service excellence
promote sustainability by adopting eco-friendly practices, reducing waste, and supporting environmentally responsible sourcing

OUR VISION

To be a globally recognized Indian exporter known for purity, traceability, and ethical trade practices in the agro and botanical products industry.

Certifications & Affiliations

Gini Enterprise complies with leading international food safety and export standards:

Certifications: FSSAI, APEDA, ISO 22000 / HACCP / GMP
Affiliations: Spices Board of India, FIEO
Testing: Independent lab testing for pesticide residues, microbial purity, curcumin levels & moisture content
